## Phase 2: Market Validation (Months 7-18)

**Assumes:** Phase 1 successful, seed round raised ($500k-1M)

### Objectives
1. Validate willingness to pay across 3 verticals
2. Build repeatable sales motion
3. Achieve break-even ($500k ARR)
4. Hire core team (Bayesian professor, engineer)
5. Launch API v1

### Key Milestones

**Month 7-9: Customer Discovery**

**Target: 3 verticals, 2 pilots each**

**Vertical 1: Journalism**
- Outreach: 20 fact-checking teams (ProPublica, Reuters, AP, local newsrooms)
- Pitch: "Reduce fact-checking time by 50%, increase confidence transparency"
- Pilot pricing: $5k for 6 months
- Goal: 2 pilots signed ($10k revenue)

**Vertical 2: Health Tech**
- Outreach: 20 supplement/wellness apps (MyFitnessPal, Examine.com, Healthline)
- Pitch: "Evidence-based content moderation, reduce liability"
- Pilot pricing: $10k for 6 months (API access)
- Goal: 2 pilots signed ($20k revenue)

**Vertical 3: Policy Think Tanks**
- Outreach: 20 think tanks (Brookings, AEI, Cato, state-level)

- Pilot pricing: $15k for 6 months
- Goal: 2 pilots signed ($30k revenue)

**AI integration:**
- ChatGPT plugin (query Legis Ledger from ChatGPT)
- Perplexity integration (cite Legis Ledger in answers)
- LLM fine-tuning (use abstracts as training data)

**Government agencies:**

## Metrics Dashboard (Ongoing)

### North Star Metrics

**Business Health:**
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV/CAC ratio (target: >3)

**Product Adoption:**
- Abstracts in corpus (quantity)
- Abstracts accessed (quality signal)
- API queries per month
- Active users (DAU/MAU)
- Threshold preferences (what % users choose)

**Sales Efficiency:**
- Pipeline size ($ value)
- Close rate (demos ‚Üí customers)
- Sales cycle length (days)
- Average contract value (ACV)
- Expansion revenue (upsells)

**Customer Satisfaction:**
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT)
- Support tickets (volume, resolution time)
- Feature requests (what customers want)

### Leading Indicators (Early Warnings)

**Growth slowing:**
- Website traffic declining
- Demo requests decreasing
- Conversion rates dropping

**Retention risk:**
- Usage declining (queries per customer)
- Support tickets increasing
- NPS dropping below 40

**Product-market fit:**
- Organic growth (word-of-mouth)
- High NPS (>50)
- Customers requesting more topics
