Like a sponge that absorbs more or less depending on how tight you squeeze it, The Certainty Scrubber filters claims based on evidence strength:

  • 90-95% (Tight filter): Only canonical knowledge passes through (rickets prevention, statins for heart disease)
  • 70-89% (Moderate filter): Well-established claims with strong evidence (vitamin D for fractures in elderly)
  • 50-69% (Loose filter): Moderate evidence, genuine uncertainty (minimum wage effects)
  • Below 50%: Speculative or highly contested claims
